# Billing and Plans

OpsPilot offers three subscription plans:

- **Free** — $0/month. Up to 100 queries per month and 1 user.
- **Pro** — $49/month. Up to 10,000 queries per month, 5 users, and email support.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing. Unlimited queries, unlimited users, SSO, and a
  dedicated account manager.

Billing happens on the same calendar day each month. Annual billing saves **20%** compared
to paying monthly.

You can upgrade at any time and the change applies immediately, with a prorated charge for
the rest of the billing period. Downgrades take effect at the **start of the next billing
cycle**, so you keep your current features until then.

To cancel, go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel plan**. Your account stays active until the
end of the period you already paid for.
