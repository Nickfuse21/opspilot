# Resetting Your Password

If you cannot sign in, you can reset your password yourself:

1. Go to the sign-in page and click **"Forgot password?"**.
2. Enter the email address on your account.
3. Open the reset email (it arrives within a few minutes) and click the secure link.
4. Choose a new password. It must be at least **12 characters** and include one number
   and one symbol.

The reset link **expires after 60 minutes**. If it expires, simply request a new one.

If no reset email arrives, check your spam folder first. Accounts using **single sign-on
(SSO)** cannot use this flow — those users must reset their password through their company
identity provider instead.
