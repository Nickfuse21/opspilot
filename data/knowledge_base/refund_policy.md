# Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method and take **5 to 7 business days** to appear.

To be eligible for a refund:
- The request must be made within the 30-day window.
- For physical goods, the item must be returned unused and in its original packaging.
- Digital subscriptions are refundable only if used for fewer than 10 days.

Orders paid by bank transfer are refunded by bank transfer only; we cannot refund a bank
transfer to a card.

After 30 days, purchases are non-refundable, but customers on an annual plan may request
account credit at the support team's discretion.
