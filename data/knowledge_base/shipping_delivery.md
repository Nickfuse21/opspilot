# Shipping and Delivery

We offer three shipping options at checkout:

- **Standard** — 3 to 5 business days, free on orders over $50.
- **Express** — 2 business days, flat $12.
- **Overnight** — next business day if ordered before 2 PM, $25.

Orders are processed Monday to Friday. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays begin
processing the next business day.

We currently ship to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. International
customers are responsible for any customs duties.

Once an order ships, a tracking number is emailed automatically. Tracking can take up to
**24 hours** to show movement after the label is created.
